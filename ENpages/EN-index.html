A gathering of VR/AR global leaders

The first GVRC with the topic of “New World innovation” will be host at Jinqiao China Europe International Business college on June 4th and 5th. The two-day conference includes forums with themes of technology, industrial applications and investment, more than 20 outstanding projects’ pitch from China and abroad, and a exhibition of current technology and products in the world. Aim at promoting the effective integration of virtual reality and traditional industries, and achieve industrial innovation.

The summit forum connecting industry representative enterprises, investment and industry sector

The business leaders: Oculus, Sony, Google, HTC, Samsung and Meta; the giants of actively involved in industrial distribution: Alibaba, Tencent, Baidu, Xiaomi, LeTV; outstanding domestic start-ups; the representatives of investing companies which are interested in this industry; the innovative companies covering medical, education, travel, home, Ecommerse , real estate , entertainment and other industrial applications; the competent department heads of relevant government sectors and media attend the forum.

Global Virtual reality conference

This conference is guided by the Shanghai Economic and Information Technology Commission, People's Government of Shanghai Pudong New Area, and co-sponsored by the Jinqiao Test Authority of the China (Shanghai) Free Trade Zone Management Committee, Shanghai Jinqiao Economic and Technological Development Management Committee , CEIBS , Shanghai Jinqiao Export Processing Zone Development Co., Ltd. and China Mobile Migu Video Technology Co., Ltd.
